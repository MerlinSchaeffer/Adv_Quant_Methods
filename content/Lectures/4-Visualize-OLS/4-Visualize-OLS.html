Lecture slides: Lecture 4

Reading: Veaux, Velleman, and Bock (2021, Ch. 9.5).

In this lecture, we will pick up some topics that we left unfinished in the prior two lectures (e.g. categorical predictors) and after that put a special focus on the basics of how to effective visualize regression results in a more appealing way than the ugly regression tables that we’re used to.

References

Veaux, De, Velleman, and Bock. 2021. Stats: Data and Models, Global Edition. Pearson Higher Ed.
